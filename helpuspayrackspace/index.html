Let me tell you a little about us...
kahub is a completely unfunded project, run out of the dorm rooms of 3 Franklin & Marshall College students. We're paying for this entire thing completely out of our wallets because we really believe that there is a better, more social, and more fun way to read the news. Although we do have one ad, that really doesn't cut it. At the end of the day, we need to pay our bills. Instead of being beholden to a few investors, or put a hell of a lot of ads on our site, we'd much rather be a project by and for the largest, bestest investor in the world. The internet.

If 2,000 people of people pitch in just $3 every month, we can keep this site humming.
